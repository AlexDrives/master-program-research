# 康奈尔大学 Cornell - Computer Science MS / MEng 课程介绍

> 来源：Cornell CS、Cornell Tech Student & Academic Affairs、Cornell Class Roster / Courses of Study 官方页面  
> 核验日期：2026-07-06  
> 覆盖项目：M.S. in Computer Science、M.Eng. in Computer Science (Ithaca)、M.Eng. in Computer Science (Cornell Tech)

---

## 项目概况

- **M.S. in Computer Science (Ithaca)**：研究型硕士，核心是 thesis research、outside minor 和 faculty committee，需完成 CS 7999 thesis research、M exam / thesis presentation 等要求。
- **M.Eng. in Computer Science (Ithaca)**：两学期全日制职业型硕士，总计 30 credits，强调 advanced technical courses 与 CS 5999 M.Eng. Project。
- **Cornell Tech M.Eng. in Computer Science**：纽约校区一年制项目，总计 30 credits，由 technical courses、Studio courses、general electives 组成；另有 AI for Engineers certificate 课程池，但证书不属于学位正式 transcript / diploma。

## 项目一：M.S. in Computer Science (Ithaca)

### 修读要求

| 项目 | 官方要求 |
|---|---|
| 总学分 | 34 credits |
| CS coursework | 至少 14 CS coursework credits |
| CS 课程层级 | CS courses 必须为 4000+；至少 4 门 CS courses 为 5000+；至少 1 门为 6000+ |
| Outside minor | 需要 outside minor，通常 2-3 门课，并由 non-CS minor member 进入 committee |
| Thesis research | CS 7999，6-12 credits；可分多学期修读 |
| Thesis grade | CS 7999 必须 letter grade，且 B or better 才能计入 thesis research requirement |
| Thesis / oral | Thesis 通常 20-50 页；需要 30-50 分钟 oral presentation / M exam |
| Good standing | 累计 GPA 至少 3.0；第 1/2/3 学期后通常需分别完成至少 8/16/24 credits |
| TA | 需在担任 TA 的课程中被评价为 strong TA |

### 官方课程结构 / 全部课程池

Cornell MS CS 官方 academic planning 页面给出的是**规则型课程结构**，而不是一个固定 elective 清单：

| 模块 | 课程 / 规则 |
|---|---|
| CS coursework | 至少 14 credits CS coursework；CS 课程需 4000+，其中至少 4 门 5000+，至少 1 门 6000+ |
| Outside minor | 由 minor field 课程组成，通常 2-3 门；需 committee minor member 批准 |
| Thesis research | CS 7999 thesis research, 6-12 credits |
| Remaining credits | 与 adviser / committee 共同规划，满足 Cornell Graduate School 和 CS field 要求 |

## 项目二：M.Eng. in Computer Science (Ithaca)

### 修读要求

| 项目 | 官方要求 |
|---|---|
| 总学分 | 30 credits；不得用于其他 degree |
| 学期上限 | 每学期最多 20 credits |
| Letter grade | 至少 28 credits 必须 letter graded；最多 2 S/U credits 计入学位 |
| Audit | Audit 不计入学位；每学期最多 1 门 audit |
| 课程层级 | 所有 non-project credits 必须同时为 technical 和 advanced；CS advanced 通常为 5000+ |
| 4000-level | CS 4000-level 不接受计入 MEng，summer 2021 前已修课程除外；co-listed 课程需选 5000-level 版本 |
| CS course credits | 至少 15 credits 来自 CS courses，不含 seminars / colloquia、independent study、CS 5998、CS 5999 project |
| Project | 3-6 credits CS 5999 Master of Engineering Project；project grade 必须 B or higher；最多 6 project credits 计入 30 credits |
| 最低成绩 | 低于 C- 的课程不计入；project 所有关联学分需 B or higher |
| GPA | 累计 GPA 至少 2.5 |
| 注册状态 | 全日制，12-credit minimum；通常两学期完成；不允许 part-time study |

### 官方课程结构 / 全部课程池

| 模块 | Credits | 官方规则 |
|---|---:|---|
| Advanced technical courses | 24-27 | 所有 non-project credits 必须 technical + advanced；CS 课程需 5000+；College of Engineering、Math、Physics、Chemistry、Johnson Information Systems 等部分 5000/6000 课程可视为 advanced，是否 technical 由 M.Eng. Curriculum Committee 判定 |
| Required CS course credits | 至少 15 | 来自 CS courses，不含 7000 seminars / colloquia、CS 5998、CS 5999、independent study |
| M.Eng. Project | 3-6 | CS 5999；需 authorized CS faculty / researcher 监督；需 final written report；B or higher |
| S/U | 最多 2 | 只有 2 S/U credits 可计入 degree requirement |

### M.Eng. Project 官方类别

- Joining a faculty member's research group.
- Further developing a project started within an advanced course, with substantial new content after the course is completed.
- Working one-on-one with a faculty member.
- Working as a member of a large College team effort.
- Collaborating with another Engineering department's M.Eng., PhD or faculty program.
- Providing critical CS skills to projects across Cornell.
- Working on commercial, industrial or government projects with appropriate NDA coordination.
- Working with students, typically CS or Johnson, on exercises that may become start-ups.

## 项目三：Cornell Tech M.Eng. in Computer Science

### 修读要求

| 项目 | 官方要求 |
|---|---|
| 总学分 | 30 credits |
| 全日制注册 | Full-time master's students 每学期至少 12 credits；建议平均 15 credits；超过 18 credits 需 Program Director approval |
| GPA | 至少 2.5；不在 good standing 的学生可能被要求离开 |
| Letter grade | 至少 28 credits letter graded；最多 2 S/U credits 计入 degree |
| 最低成绩 | 只有 C- 或以上课程计入学位 |
| Studio grade | TECH 5900 或 TECH 5910 / 5920 / 5930 必须 B or higher |
| Audit / CPT | Audit 不计入；每学期最多 1 门 audit；TECH 5999 CPT 不计入 graduation / degree requirements |

### 2026-2027 Curriculum - 官方课程结构

| 模块 | Credits | 官方课程 / 规则 |
|---|---:|---|
| Technical Courses | 18 | 5 CS courses = 15 credits；Technical Electives = 3 credits，从 CS、ORIE、ECE、INFO offerings 中选择 |
| Studio Courses | 8 | TECH 5900 Product Studio (4)；TECH 5910 Startup Studio 或 TECH 5920 BigCo Studio 或 TECH 5930 PiTech Impact Studio (3)；TECH Studio Elective (1)。TECHIE prefix classes 不算 Studio electives |
| General Electives | 4 | 从 Cornell Tech campus offerings 中选择：CS、DESIGN、ECE、ORIE、INFO、LAW、NBAY、TECH、TECHIE |

### AI for Engineers Certificate - 官方课程池

证书不是正式 university degree 的一部分，不出现在 diploma 或 transcript；但 Cornell Tech 官方 curriculum 页面给出 AI-focused course selection。要求至少选择 3 门下列课程，且至少 1 门带 *，其他课程需 Program Director permission；另选 1 门 policy / ethics / law / healthcare / optimization 课程。

| 类别 | 官方课程池 |
|---|---|
| AI technical options | CS 5785 Applied Machine Learning (*)；CS 5781 Machine Learning Engineering (*)；CS 5787 Deep Learning (*)；CS 5788 Intro to Generative Models；CS 5789 Introduction to Reinforcement Learning；CS 5744 Language Modeling；CS 5670 Computer Vision；CS 5434 Trustworthy AI |
| Additional required option | LAW 6146 AI Law and Policy；LAW 6336 Law and Regulation of Artificial Intelligence；CS 5382 / INFO 5390 Fairness in Machine Learning；INFO 5600 AI for Healthcare；INFO 5375 Health Tech-Oriented Machine Learning；ORIE 5320 Optimization for AI |

## 官方课程描述抓手

- Cornell MS CS 与 Ithaca MEng 的具体课程描述以 Cornell Courses of Study 和 Class Roster 为准；项目页面给出的主要是 credit / level / grade / project rules。
- Cornell Tech MEng 的课程结构以 Student & Academic Affairs curriculum 页面为准，课程描述入口为 Cornell University Registrar。
- 机器人 / 感知相关可从官方 roster / catalog 检索：CS 5750 Foundations of Robotics、CS 5670 Computer Vision、CS 4789 / CS 6789 Reinforcement Learning、CS 5787 Deep Learning、CS 5789 Introduction to Reinforcement Learning、CS 5744 Language Modeling 等。
