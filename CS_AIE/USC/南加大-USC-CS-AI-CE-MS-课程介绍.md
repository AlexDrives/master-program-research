# 南加州大学 USC - CS / CS-AI / Computer Engineering MS 课程介绍

> 来源：USC Thomas Lord Department of Computer Science、USC Viterbi Graduate Admission、USC Catalogue / Schedule of Classes 官方页面  
> 核验日期：2026-07-06  
> 覆盖项目：M.S. in Computer Science (General)、M.S. in Computer Science - Artificial Intelligence、M.S. in Computer Engineering

---

## 项目概况

- **MS Computer Science (General)**：通用计算机科学硕士，强调数字计算机设计、编程和应用的基础概念与技术。Fall 2024 起非论文路径为 32 units；论文路径为 36 units。
- **MS Computer Science - Artificial Intelligence**：CS 专门方向硕士，训练 deep learning、AI、machine learning、statistics、computer vision、NLP、robotics 等理论与应用。总学分 32 units。
- **MS Computer Engineering**：由 ECE 管理，覆盖硬件与软件系统、组件、网络的设计、开发、测试与评估；重点研究方向为 Computer Architecture and Parallel Processing、Design and Analysis of Computer Networks and their Protocols、VLSI Design and CAD / Verification of VLSI。项目要求至少 28 units。

## 项目一：M.S. in Computer Science (General)

### 修读要求

| 项目 | 官方要求 |
|---|---|
| 总学分 | Fall 2024 起 coursework track 为 32 units；旧页面仍保留 Total Units: 28 的历史说明 |
| 核心课 | CSCI 570 Analysis of Algorithms (4) |
| 其他课程 | 其余学分主要从 500-level CSCI courses 中选择 |
| 400/600-level 限制 | 最多 4 units 可来自批准的 400-level EE 或 CSCI；最多两门 doctoral 600-level course 可计入学位 |
| Non-CS | 最多 1 门经批准的 non-CS course 可计入 |
| Research | CSCI 590 Directed Research 最多 2 units；CSCI 591 Computer Science Research Colloquium 最多 2 units |
| 不计入 elective | CSCI 594a/b/z Thesis；ENGR 595a/b/z Internship |
| GPA / 考试 | GPA 至少 3.00；无 cumulative examination |
| Thesis option | 需找到指导老师并获批；Fall 2024 起总学分 36 units，含至少两个学期 CSCI 594a/b/z，最多 4 units thesis |

### 官方课程结构 / 全部课程池

| 模块 | 课程 / 规则 |
|---|---|
| Core Requirement | CSCI 570 Analysis of Algorithms (4) |
| Additional 500-level CSCI | Additional 500-level CSCI courses |
| Approved non-CS | One non Computer Science Course, such as EE / ISE / INF, from approved list, 3-4 units |
| Doctoral CSCI | 600-level CSCI course with faculty and department approval, max 8 units |
| Research options | CSCI 590 Directed Research；CSCI 591 Computer Science Research Colloquium |
| Thesis option | CSCI 594a / CSCI 594b / CSCI 594z, only for approved thesis track |

## 项目二：M.S. in Computer Science - Artificial Intelligence

### 修读要求

| 项目 | 官方要求 |
|---|---|
| 总学分 | 32 units |
| Required courses | 20 units：5 门必修 |
| Group electives | 3 courses；三个 group 每组至少 1 门 |
| Thesis / internship | Thesis 和 internship credits 不能计入 elective units；该专业方向页面未列 thesis option |
| Directed / special research | CSCI 599 或 CSCI 699 经系批准可用于 elective group requirements；替代申请只在学生最后一学期审核 |
| GPA / 考试 | GPA 至少 3.00；无 cumulative examination |

### Required Courses (20 units)

| 代码 | 课程名 | Units |
|---|---|---:|
| CSCI 570 | Analysis of Algorithms | 4 |
| CSCI 561 | Foundations of Artificial Intelligence | 4 |
| CSCI 566 | Deep Learning and Its Applications | 4 |
| CSCI 567 | Machine Learning | 4 |
| CSCI 571 | Web Technologies | 4 |

### Group Electives - 完整官方课程池

| Group | 要求 | 官方课程池 |
|---|---|---|
| Group 1 - Machine Learning and Deep Learning | 至少 1 门 | EE 546 Mathematics of High-Dimensional Data；EE 588 Optimization for the Information and Data Sciences；ISE 633 Large Scale Optimization and Machine Learning；CSCI 535 Multimodal Probabilistic Learning of Human Communication；CSCI 559 Machine Learning I: Supervised Methods；CSCI 563 Building Knowledge Graphs；CSCI 583 Machine Learning Theory；CSCI 659 Introduction to Online Optimization；CSCI 663 Artificial Intelligence for Social Good；CSCI 668 Search and Planning；CSCI 675 Convex and Combinatorial Optimization；CSCI 678 Theoretical Machine Learning |
| Group 2 - Natural Language Processing and Speech Recognition | 至少 1 门 | CSCI 544 Applied Natural Language Processing；CSCI 662 Advanced Natural Language Processing；EE 519 Speech Recognition and Processing for Multimedia |
| Group 3 - Computer Vision and Robotics | 至少 1 门 | CSCI 445L Foundations of Robotics；CSCI 545 Robotics；CSCI 677 Advanced Computer Vision；EE 569 Introduction to Digital Image Processing |

说明：USC 官方页面注明 CSCI 445 / 445L 必须先于 CSCI 545 修读。

## 项目三：M.S. in Computer Engineering

### 修读要求

| 项目 | 官方要求 |
|---|---|
| 总学分 | 至少 28 units approved course work |
| CE coursework | 至少 20 units 来自 Computer Engineering coursework |
| Area coverage | Architecture、Networks、VLSI/CAD 三个 area 中至少覆盖两个 area，且各至少 1 门 |
| Foundations | Foundations area 课程也计入 20-unit Computer Engineering requirement |
| Thesis / Directed Research | 第一学期后可申请 thesis option 或 Directed Research；不保证获批 |
| STEM / DEN | 项目 eligible for OPT STEM extension；DEN@Viterbi 与校内 degree 等价 |

### 官方课程结构 / 全部课程池

| Area | 官方课程 |
|---|---|
| Computer Architecture Area | EE 457 Computer Systems Organization (4)；EE 557 Computer Systems Architecture (4) |
| Networks Area | EE 450 Introduction to Computer Networks (4)；EE 555 Broadband Network Architectures (3) |
| VLSI/CAD Area | EE 477L MOS VLSI Circuit Design (4)；EE 536a Mixed-Signal Integrated Circuit Design (4)；EE 552 Asynchronous VLSI Design (3)；EE 577a VLSI System Design (3) |
| Foundations | CSCI 402 Operating Systems (4)；EE 503 Probability for Electrical and Computer Engineers (4)；EE 510 Linear Algebra for Engineering (4)；EE 660 Machine Learning from Signals: Foundations and Methods (3) |
